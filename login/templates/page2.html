TYPES OF STUDY-MODE WE OFFER

We understand how devastating one can feel when studying alone or when studying with friends. Surely
everyone has their own preferences on how they study their subjects material.
Thus, Connectify here offers you three types of study-mode

1.STUDY-ALONE

Study-alone mode fosters deep focus and self-discipline. It allows for personalized learning environments, free from distractions, and helps in developing independent problem-solving skills. This solitary approach often leads to better retention and understanding of complex subjects.

2.STUDY-BUDDY

Studying with friends encourages collaborative learning and diverse perspectives, making complex topics more accessible. It promotes mutual support, motivation, and accountability, while also making study sessions more engaging and less monotonous. This social approach can enhance comprehension and retention.

STUDY-WITH-RANDOMCOURSEMATES

Studying with random coursemates introduces diverse viewpoints and study techniques, fostering a richer learning experience. It promotes adaptability and teamwork, as students navigate different study styles. This approach can enhance understanding and make studying more dynamic and interactive.
