Study-Alone Mode

The "Study Alone" mode on Connectify allows students to focus individually while still being connected to others. This feature promotes a distraction-free environment, helping students stay productive and motivated while studying independently within the Connectify platform.

Study-Buddy Mode

The "Study-Buddy" mode on Connectify enables students to pair up with a study partner, fostering collaboration and mutual support. This feature encourages interactive learning, making study sessions more engaging and effective by allowing students to share knowledge and stay motivated together.

Study-With-Random-Coursemate

The "Study with Random Coursemates" mode on Connectify connects students with peers taking the same course, offering an opportunity to collaborate with new classmates. This feature enhances learning by promoting diverse perspectives and fostering a sense of community within the course.
