CODING

The foundation-level coding curriculum covers computing basics, including computer concepts, programming, problem-solving with algorithms, and real-world projects. It builds a strong foundation in hardware, software, and teamwork, preparing students for advanced studies.

PRE-CALCULUS

The foundation-level precalculus curriculum includes Maths 1 (fundamental algebra), Maths 2 (trigonometry), and Maths 3 (analytic geometry and pre-calculus). These subjects build a strong mathematical foundation, preparing students for calculus and higher-level studies in related fields.

COMPUTER-ENGINEERING

The foundation-level computer engineering curriculum covers digital logic, circuit design, and essential physics concepts like mechanics and electromagnetism. These subjects provide the foundational knowledge needed for understanding and advancing in computer engineering.
